# Human Resources Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository features an interactive, 2-page **Human Resources Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**. The project analyzes the comprehensive **IBM HR Analytics Dataset** (Attrition & Performance) to uncover the core behavioral, financial, and demographic drivers behind employee turnover.

The primary objective of this business intelligence solution is to deliver actionable insights to HR leaders, enabling data-driven decision-making to mitigate risk and optimize talent retention.

---

## 📊 Key Dashboards & Visual Insights

### 1. General Workforce Overview
* **High-Level KPIs:** Instant visibility into total headcount (1,470 employees), average monthly salary ($6,503), average workforce age (36.92 years), and average tenure (7.01 years).
* **Demographic Distributions:** Deep dive into the active workforce broken down by precise Age brackets, Education levels, Departments, and Gender.

### 2. Attrition & Behavioral Analysis
* **The Attrition Rate:** Comprehensive tracking of the company's **16.12%** attrition baseline (representing 237 departed employees).
* **Burnout & Workload Indicators:** Explores the severe correlation between **Overtime hours** and turnover rates.
* **Role-Specific Vulnerabilities:** Clear comparison identifying high-risk job roles (such as *Sales Representatives* and *Laboratory Technicians*) that experience above-average attrition percentages.
* **Travel & Marital Demographics:** Analysis of how frequent *Business Travel* and *Marital Status* cross-reference with an employee's likelihood to leave the organization.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Data Engineering Methodology
* **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
* **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics (Attrition & Performance)
* **Data Cleansing & Transformation (Power Query):** Structured raw inputs, handled missing dimensions, aligned data types, and optimized the dataset for continuous analysis.
* **Calculations & Analytics (Advanced DAX):** Engineered custom dynamic measures for accurate Attrition Rates, active employee tracking, and descriptive averages.
* **UI/UX Design Principles:** Implemented a clean, modern grid layout using a custom professional color palette, structured KPIs card placement, and intuitive visual hierarchies for executive-level scanning.
